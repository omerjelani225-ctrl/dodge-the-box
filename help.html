How to Play

Quick rules and controls

Goal

Survive each stage by dodging the falling blocks. Clear 100 stages to win.

Controls

  • Keyboard: ← → or A D
  • Mobile: hold the on screen left/right buttons

Enemy types

  • Normal: falls straight down
  • Burst: sometimes speeds up suddenly
  • Zigzag: drifts left and right while falling
  • Heavy: appears after a warning and falls faster

Tips

  • Stay near the center when a new stage starts.
  • Small moves beat big panicked moves.
  • If you see a warning marker, prepare to dodge a heavy drop.
